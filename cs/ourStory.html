Our Story

A Legacy Stitched with Love

I am Jincy, daughter of Sheeja and Christin.

CS Stitchings didn’t begin with a grand plan—but with the quiet strength of a mother, and the steadfast support of a father. In a modest room, with just a sewing machine and a heart full of hope, my mother stitched day and night—not just garments, but a future for her family. By her side, my father stood strong—helping with every step, every struggle, every small victory.

Their efforts weren’t about building a brand. They were about building a life. Every saree altered, every blouse tailored, was more than a job—it was an act of love. A silent promise.
A legacy in the making.

I grew up watching those gentle hands and steady shoulders create beauty out of simplicity. Inspired by the grace, grit, and unity behind every stitch, I dreamed of carrying their spirit forward—not just to run a business,but to build something meaningful.

And so, CS Stitchings was born—a tribute to a mother’s art, a father’s strength, and a daughter’s dream.

Today, we stand for more than fashion. We stand for trust, elegance, and stories woven into every fold of fabric. Every design reflects care. Every stitch echoes the love that started it all.

When you choose CS Stitchings, you're not just choosing clothing— You're becoming part of a story stitched with soul.

From a single, a legacy was born.

Rooted in love, built by a family, crafted with care—CS Stitchings isn’t just fashion. It’s a story of strength, simplicity, and soul.
